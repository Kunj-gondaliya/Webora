What is Dark Mode & Neumorphism?

Dark mode interfaces use darker backgrounds with light text, reducing eye strain and creating a sleek look. Neumorphism adds soft, 3D-like effects to UI elements, giving depth and modernity.

Why it’s Popular in 2025

Users enjoy the aesthetic and reduced glare of dark mode. Neumorphic designs provide a fresh, tactile feeling to buttons, cards, and other elements, enhancing UX.

How to Implement

Combine dark backgrounds with bright accent colors. Use subtle shadows and highlights for neumorphic buttons and cards. Keep it minimal to avoid clutter.
