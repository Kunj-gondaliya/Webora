Case Study: FitGear Hub

Challenge

FitGear Hub, a mid-sized gym equipment supplier, had a basic website but faced very high bounce rates (customers leaving without buying) and cart abandonment rates over 70%. The site loaded slowly, had a confusing checkout process, and no live support. Their competitors with sleeker websites were eating into their market share.

Solution

• Webora rebuilt their platform from the ground up.
• Boosted page speed by 40% with optimized images and server caching.
• Designed a mobile-first UI since 60% of their traffic came from phones.
• Revamped the checkout process, reducing 6 steps into just 3.
• Integrated a 24/7 AI-powered chatbot to handle FAQs
• Added customer testimonials & video demos to build trust .

Results

• Cart abandonment dropped from 70% to 28% in just 2 months.
• Conversions increased by 112%, generating ₹5 lakh extra revenue per quarter.
• Customers loved the chatbot, with 83% using it before making a purchase.
• FitGear Hub now ranks on Google’s some of the best page for “buy gym equipment online India”.
