How SEO Impacts Your Website

Why Search Engine Optimization matters for growth

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving your website’s visibility in search engines like Google. A well-optimized site ranks higher, attracts more organic visitors, and builds trust with customers.

1. Visibility & Ranking

Higher ranking means more traffic. The first page of Google captures 71% of search traffic clicks.

2. Credibility & Trust

Users trust websites that appear at the top of search results. Good SEO improves brand authority.

3. Better User Experience

SEO requires mobile-friendliness, fast loading, and quality content—all improving UX.
